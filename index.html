Why Invest Here

Malaysia's Property
Advantages

01
Competitive Property Prices
Kuala Lumpur offers world-class luxury properties at a fraction of the cost compared to Singapore, Hong Kong, or London — delivering exceptional value and capital appreciation potential.
02
No Capital Gains Tax
Foreign investors benefit from Malaysia's favourable tax environment — no capital gains tax for properties held beyond 5 years, maximising your net returns on investment.
03
Freehold Ownership
Foreigners may own freehold properties in Malaysia with full title, ensuring long-term asset security and generational wealth transfer — unlike leasehold restrictions in many neighbouring countries.
04
Strategic ASEAN Location
Malaysia sits at the heart of ASEAN — a rapidly growing economic bloc of 680 million people. KL is a global business hub with excellent connectivity to the world's major cities.
05
Strong Rental Yields
Prime KL properties consistently generate gross rental yields of 4–6% per annum, underpinned by a large expatriate community, digital nomads, and growing regional demand.
06
Transparent Legal Framework
Malaysia operates under English common law. The property purchase process is clear, professionally regulated, and designed to protect buyers — giving investors full confidence and peace of mind.

Malaysia My Second Home

Residency Programme

Live in Malaysia
with MM2H

The Malaysia My Second Home (MM2H) programme is a government initiative that allows foreigners to live in Malaysia on a long-term, renewable social visit pass of up to 20 years. It is one of the most attractive residency programmes in Southeast Asia.

20-Year Renewable VisaLong-term stay with multiple-entry privileges for the whole family.
Bring Your FamilySpouse, children under 21, and parents are eligible as dependants.
Import a Car Tax-FreeOne personal vehicle may be imported duty-free upon approval.
Work PermissionApproved holders may apply for limited part-time work permits.

Key Requirements
at a Glance

  • Offshore Monthly IncomeRM 40,000+
  • Fixed Deposit (Applicant)RM 1,000,000
  • Liquid AssetsRM 1,500,000+
  • Minimum Stay Required90 days / year
  • Property PurchaseMin. RM 1,000,000
  • Visa Duration20 years (renewable)
